[LG4240] 毒瘤之神的考验

发表于 2020-05-29 更新于 2020-06-05 分类于 题解

link

首先有式子 \[ \begin{aligned} \varphi(ab) &= ab\prod_{i=1}(1-\frac{1}{p_i}) \\ &=ab\prod_{p \mid a}(1-\frac{1}{p})\prod_{p \mid b}(1-\frac{1}{p})\frac{1}{\prod_{p\mid \gcd(a,b)}(1-\frac{1}{p})} \\ &=\frac{\varphi(a)\varphi(b)}{\frac{\varphi(\gcd(a,b))}{\gcd(a,b)}} \\ &=\frac{\varphi(a)\varphi(b)\gcd(a,b)}{\varphi(\gcd(a,b))} \end{aligned} \] 枚举 \(\gcd\) . \[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m\varphi(ij) &= \sum_{d=1}^n\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m\varphi(i)\varphi(j)\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}[(i,j)=d] \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^n\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m\varphi(i)\varphi(j)[(i,j)=d] \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^n\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{d} \rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{m}{d} \rfloor}\varphi(id)\varphi(jd)[(i,j)=1] \\ &= \sum_{d=1}^n\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\sum_{k=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{k} \rfloor}\mu(k)\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{dk} \rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{dk} \rfloor}\varphi(idk)\varphi(jdk) \\ &=\sum_{T=1}^n\left(\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor \frac{n}{T} \rfloor }\varphi(iT)\right)\left(\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{m}{T} \rfloor }\varphi(jT)\right)\sum_{d\mid T}\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\mu(\frac{T}{d}) \end{aligned} \] 后面很好求 , 现在问题是中间两个和式怎么求.

令 \(G(x,y)= \sum_{i=1}^{x} \varphi(iy)\) , 于是有 \[ \begin{aligned} G(x,y) &= G(x-1,y) + \varphi(xy) \end{aligned} \] 由于 \(xy \leq 10^5\) , 总共只有 \(n \ln n\) 个合法状态 , 可以直接求出.

设

\[ \begin{aligned} T(n,a,b) = \sum_{T=1}^n\left(\sum_{i=1}^{a}\varphi(iT)\right)\left(\sum_{j=1}^{b}\varphi(jT)\right)\sum_{d\mid T}\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\mu(\frac{T}{d}) \end{aligned} \]

于是有 \[ \begin{aligned} T(n,a,b) &= T(n-1,a,b)+\left(\sum_{d\mid n}\frac{d}{\varphi(d)}\mu(\frac{n}{d})\right)G(a,n)G(b,n) \end{aligned} \] 根据整除分块 , 可以求出 \(T(1 \sim n , a,b)\) 的前缀和 , 每次只需用 \(T( \text{up} , a , b ) - T( \text{down} - 1 , a , b )\) .

问题是 \(T\) 的大小是 \(n^2 \ln n\) , 考虑只求出一部分的值. 设阈值 \(B\) , 只求出 \(T(n,a,b) , a,b\leq B\) , 此时复杂度是 \(O(nB^2)\) , 然后对于 \(\lfloor \frac{n}{T}\rfloor > B\) 的情况 , 有 \(T \leq \lfloor \frac{n}{B} \rfloor\) , 于是可以 \(O(\frac{n}{B})\) 直接算. 对于 \(\lfloor \frac{n}{T}\rfloor \leq B\) 的情况 , 直接整除分块 , 每次询问的复杂度就是 \(O(\sqrt n + \lfloor \frac{n}{B} \rfloor )\) . \(B\) 取到 \(35\) 就差不多了.
