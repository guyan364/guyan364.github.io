类欧几里得算法

发表于 2020-03-30 更新于 2020-04-08 分类于 学习笔记

以 \(O(\log n)\) 的时间复杂度解决一类求和问题

\(\sum_{i=0}^n\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor\)

设 \(f(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^n\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor\)

当 \(a\geq c\) 或 \(b \geq c\) 时

\[ \begin{aligned} f(a,b,c,n) &= \sum_{i=0}^n \lfloor \frac{\left( \lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor \cdot c + (a\bmod c) \right) i+\lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor \cdot c + (b\bmod c)}{c} \rfloor \\ &= \sum_{i=0}^n \frac{ \lfloor \frac{a}{c}\rfloor \cdot c \cdot i + \lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor \cdot c}{c} + \sum_{i=0}^n \lfloor \frac{(a\bmod c)i + (b\bmod c)}{c} \rfloor \\ &= \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor + ( n + 1 ) \lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor + f( a \bmod c , b \bmod c , c , n ) \end{aligned} \] 这样就可以将 \(a \geq c\) 或 \(b \geq c\) 的情况化成 \(a < c\) 且 \(b < c\) 的情况

当 \(a < c\) 且 \(b < c\) 时

\(f(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^n \lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor\)

设 \(m = \lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor\) \[ f(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^{m-1} \sum_{j=0}^n[\lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c}\rfloor > i] \] 考虑后面的 \([\lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c}\rfloor > i]\)

\(\lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c}\rfloor > i \Leftrightarrow \lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c}\rfloor \geq i + 1\)

\(aj+b \geq ci + c\)

\(aj + b > ci + c - 1\)

\(j > \lfloor \frac{ci+c-1-b}{a} \rfloor\)

\(f(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^m \sum_{j=0}^n[j > \lfloor \frac{ci+c-1-b}{a} \rfloor]\)

\(f(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^m n - \sum_{i=0}^m\lfloor \frac{ci+c-1-b}{a} \rfloor\)

\(f(a,b,c,n) = mn - f(c,c-b-1,a,m-1)\)

这里 \(a\) , \(c\) 交换了位置 , 那么由于 \(a < c\) , 下一步又会进行取模

类似于辗转相除 , 故时间复杂度是 \(O(\log n)\)

\(\sum_{i=0}^n i \lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor\)

当 \(a \geq c\) 或 \(b \geq c\) 时

\[ \begin{aligned} g(a,b,c,n) &= \sum_{i=0}^n i^2 \lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor + i\lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor + g(a\bmod c,b\bmod c,c,n) \\ &= \frac{(n+1)(2n+1)n}{6}\lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor + \frac{n(n+1)}{2}\lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor + g(a\bmod c,b\bmod c,c,n) \end{aligned} \]

当 \(a < c\) 且 \(b < c\) 时

设 \(m = \lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor\)

\[ \begin{aligned} g(a,b,c,n) &= \sum_{i=0}^{m-1} \sum_{j=0}^n [\lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c} \rfloor > i] \cdot j \\ &= \sum_{i=0}^{m-1} \sum_{j=0}^n[j > \lfloor \frac{ci+c-1-b}{a} \rfloor] \cdot j \end{aligned} \]

设 \(t=\lfloor \frac{ci+c-b-1}{c} \rfloor\)

\(g(a,b,c,n) = \sum_{i=0}^{m-1} \sum_{j=0}^n [j > t] \cdot j\)

等差数列求和

\[ \begin{aligned} g(a,b,c,n) &= \sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\frac{(t+1+n)(n-t)}{2}\\ &= \frac{1}{2}\left( mn(n+1) - h(c,c-b-1,a,m - 1) - f(c,c-b-1,a,m - 1) \right) \end{aligned} \]

此处 \(h(a,b,c,n)=\sum_{i=0}^n {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor} ^ 2\)

\(\sum_{i=0}^n {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}^2\)

当 \(a \geq c\) 或 \(b \geq c\) 时

\[ \begin{aligned} h(a,b,c,n) = &h( a \bmod c , b \bmod c , c , n ) + 2\lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor g( a \bmod c , b \bmod c , c , n ) + 2\lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor f( a \bmod c , b \bmod , c , c , n ) \\ &+ {\lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor} ^ 2 \frac{n(2n+1)(n+1)}{6} + {\lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor} ^ 2(n+1) + \lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor \lfloor \frac{b}{c} \rfloor n(n+1) \end{aligned} \]

当 \(a < c\) 且 \(b < c\) 时

不直接求 \(n^2\) , 换成 \(2\frac{n(n+1)}{2}-n = \left(2\sum_{i=1}^n i\right) - n\) \[ \begin{aligned} h(a,b,c,n) &= \sum_{i=0}^n {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}^2\\ &= \sum_{i=0}^n\left(2\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c}\rfloor}j - \lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c}\rfloor\right) \\ &= 2\left(\sum_{i=0}^n \sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}j\right) - f(a,b,c,n) \end{aligned} \] 考虑减号前的式子 \[ \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=0}^n \sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}j &= \sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor - 1} (j+1) \sum_{i=0}^n\left[j<\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor \right] \\ &=\sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor - 1} (j+1) \sum_{i=0}^n\left[ i > \lfloor \frac{cj+c-b-1}{a} \rfloor \right] \\ &=\sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor - 1} (j+1) \left( n - \lfloor \frac{cj+c-b-1}{a} \rfloor\right) \\ &= \frac{\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor(\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor+1)}{2}n -g(c,c-b-1,a,\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor-1) -f(c,c-b-1,a,\lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor-1) \end{aligned} \] 设 \(m = \lfloor \frac{an+b}{c} \rfloor\) 那么得到 \[ h(a,b,c,n) = m(m+1)n - 2g(c,c-b-1,a,m-1) - 2f(c,c-b-1,a,m-1) - f(a,b,c,n) \]

更一般的形式

\(\sum_{i=0}^n i^{k_1} {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}^{k_2}\)

设 \(f( a , b , c , n , k_1 , k_2 ) = \sum_{i=0}^n i^{k_1} {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}^{k_2}\)

当 \(a \geq c\) 或 \(b \geq c\) 时

\[ \begin{aligned} f(a,b,c,n,k_1,k_2) &= \sum_{i=0}^n i ^{k_1} {\left(\left(\lfloor\frac{a}{c}\rfloor i + \lfloor \frac{b}{c}\rfloor\right)+\lfloor \frac{(a \bmod c)i + (b\bmod c)}{c} \rfloor\right)}^{k_2} \\ &= \sum_{i=0}^n i^{k_1} \sum_{j=0}^{k_2} \binom{k_2}{j} \left(\lfloor\frac{a}{c}\rfloor i + \lfloor \frac{b}{c}\rfloor\right)^j \left(\lfloor \frac{(a \bmod c)i + (b\bmod c)}{c} \rfloor \right)^{k_2-j} \\ &= \sum_{i+j+k = k_2 , i, j ,k \geq 0} \binom{k_2}{i,j,k} \left( \lfloor \frac{a}{c} \rfloor \right)^i \left(\lfloor \frac{b}{c}\rfloor\right)^jf(a\bmod c,b\bmod c,c,n,k_1 + i , k) \end{aligned} \] 其中 \(\binom{k_2}{i,j,k}\) 是三项式展开的系数

当 \(a < c\) 且 \(b < c\) 时

\[ \begin{aligned} f(a,b,c,n,k_1,k_2) &= \sum_{i=0}^n i^{k_1} {\lfloor \frac{ai+b}{c} \rfloor}^{k_2} \\ &= \sum_{i=0}^{m - 1}\left( (i+1)^{k_2} - i^{k_2} \right)\sum_{j=0}^{n} \left[i < \lfloor \frac{aj+b}{c} \rfloor\right] \cdot j^{k_1} \\ &= \sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\left((i+1)^{k_2}-i^{k_2}\right)\left(\sum_{j=0}^n j^{k_1} - \sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor\frac{ci+c-b-1}{a}\rfloor}j^{k_1}\right) \\ &=m^{k_2} \sum_{i=1}^n i^{k_1} - \sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\left((i+1)^{k_2}-i^{k_2}\right)\sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor\frac{ci+c-b-1}{a}\rfloor}j^{k_1} \end{aligned} \] 令 \(\sum_{i=0}^{k+1} s_{k,i} n^i = \sum_{i=1}^n i ^ k\) ( \(s\) 数组可以拉格朗日插值求出 ) \[ \begin{aligned} f(a,b,c,n,k_1,k_2) &= m^{k_2}\sum_{i=1}^n i ^{k_1} - \sum_{k=0}^{k_2-1}\left(\sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\binom{k_2}{k}i^k\cdot \sum_{j=0}^{\lfloor\frac{ci+c-b-1}{a}\rfloor}j^{k_1}\right) \\ &=m^{k_2}\sum_{i=1}^n i ^{k_1} - \sum_{k=0}^{k_2-1}\left(\sum_{i=0}^{m-1}\binom{k_2}{k}i^k\cdot \sum_{j=0}^{k_1+1}s_{k_1,j}\left(\lfloor \frac{ci+c-b-1}{a} \rfloor\right)^{j}\right) \\ &=m^{k_2}\sum_{i=1}^n i ^{k_1} - \sum_{k=0}^{k_2-1}\binom{k_2}{k}\sum_{j=0}^{k_1+1}s_{k_1,j}f(c,c-b-1,a,m-1,k,j) \end{aligned} \]
